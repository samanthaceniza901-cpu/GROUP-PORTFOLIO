About the Art Club

Welcome to Palettes — a creative space for artists to express, explore, and evolve. Our portfolio showcases our journey, passion, and colorful imagination throughout the school year.



This portfolio features the works of Krystanna, Reyni, Pia, and Sammy — the current members of the Palettes Art Club. Each member is driven by a deep passion for the creative arts, expressing themselves through a wide range of mediums such as illustration, photography, and video editing. Together, they combine their individual styles and ideas to create meaningful pieces that reflect their imagination, collaboration, and growth as artists. Through this collection, they aim to celebrate creativity, inspire others, and showcase the vibrant spirit of the Palettes Art Club.
