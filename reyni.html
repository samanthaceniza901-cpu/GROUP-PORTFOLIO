    Sari-sari Store ni Ming

    These illustrations are part of my storybook titled Ming's Sari-sari Store. I created it using Procreate on my iPad Air 4 as a project for the course Illustration and Cartooning (MMA155L A402). The artwork captures the everyday charm and vibrant energy of a traditional Filipino sari-sari store, highlighting its colorful products, bustling atmosphere, and the warmth of community interactions.

    Pixel Art

    Using Procreate, I created pixel art icons of various characters commissioned by my clients. This experience served as a great foundation for learning the techniques and nuances of pixel art, from working with limited color palettes to designing in small, precise spaces.
